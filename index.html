My Favorite Artists


Noah Kahan

With his folk-pop music, Noah has been my favorite artist for the past few years. I've seen his concert at Lollapalooza 2023, and I'll see him again at the Gorge in June, 2024.


Hozier

Known for his indie melodies and beautiful lyrics, Hozier is one of my favorite artists and I am excited to see his concert this year. I'll see him at Lollapalooza 2024 in August, and later at the Gorge in September.


Jao

Jao is my favorite Brazilian singer, known for sad lyrics and upbeat melodies. I've seen him three times in Sao Paulo between 2018 and 2020 and I can't wait to see another concert.
